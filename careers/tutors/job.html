Tutor

Remote, Indonesia Part-Time

Responsibility

This position is directly responsible to Curriculum and Education of TORCHE Education, and responsible for:

  1. Preparing presentation materials based on the TORCHE Education Curriculum Guide,
  2. Ready to teach online classes
  3. Commit to completing the classes as stated in the Letter of Assignment

General Qualifications

  1. Passionate in the education sector.
  2. Able to teach using online platforms (Google Meet & Zoom).
  3. Proficient in Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint) and Google Workspace (Google Drive, Google Docs, Google Sheets, and Google Slides).
  4. Excellent analytical and communication skill.
  5. Fluent in English is a plus.
  6. Students/graduates from any universities are welcome to apply.
  7. Having reliable internet connection and quite working environment

Subjects open for hiring

Basic Engineering Courses

  • Calculus for Engineering
  • Fundamentals of Physics - Mechanics and Heat
  • Fundamental of Physics - Magnets, Electrics, Waves, Optics
  • Statistics & Probability
  • Linear Algebra

Specific minimum requirements:

  • Undergraduate student in Engineering or Natural Science
  • Have passed the chosen course with a minimum score of B (3.00/4.00)

Basic Chemical & Bioprocess Engineering Courses

  • Cell Culture
  • Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics
  • Engineering Economy
  • Fluid and Particles Mechanics
  • Fundamentals of Heat Transfer
  • Fundamentals of Mass Transfer
  • Numerical Computation for Engineers
  • Transport Phenomena

Specific minimum requirements:

  • Undergraduate student in Chemical Engineering, Bioprocess Engineering, or similar
  • Have passed the chosen course with a minimum score of B (3.00/4.00)

Advanced Chemical Engineering Courses

  • Chemical Engineering Mathematics
  • Chemical Process Simulation
  • Process Control and Dynamics
  • Product Design and Development
  • Process Equipment Design
  • Process Plant Design

Specific minimum requirements:

  • Undergraduate student in Chemical Engineering, Bioprocess Engineering, or similar
  • Have passed the chosen course with a minimum score of B (3.00/4.00)
  • Specific for Chemical Process Simulation, able to operate chemical process simulator (Aspen HYSYS/Unisim is preferred)
  • Specific for Plant Design and Product Design, must be an Undergraduate Degree in Chemical/Bioprocess Engineering with the minimum score for the chosen course 3.5/4.00

Apply For The Job
