Torche Webinar Series Episode 1: The Impact of Conventional Energy Used on Life Quality and The Environment

Torche Webinar Series 2022 is a series of event held by Torche Education to raise awareness of climate change. Climate change has been a serious problem these days and nations has conclude to reduce the emission of carbon dioxide to maintain the annual temperature below 2 degrees Celsius in COP26 Glasgow. The first episode of the series discussed about how energy we used up to now has damaged the climate, environment, and the life quality of people.

About the Speakers

Ghivarly Nadief Addarquthni, S.T.

Ghivarly is a Sustainability Research Assistant at World Research Institute, focusing on sustainable energy. He graduated from Institut Teknologi Bandung majoring in chemical engineering, and an intern in Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency division at ASEAN Centre for Energy.
