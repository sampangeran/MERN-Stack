Virtual Open House 2021

Torche Virtual Open House 2021 is an event by invitation only for students’ associations from all over Indonesia. The event held to showcase Torche’s profile, services, and achievements and collaborate with students’ association. After the main event, Torche is opening free demo classes for students that attended the event. This event is attended by more than 20 student associations representatives from universities around Indonesia.
