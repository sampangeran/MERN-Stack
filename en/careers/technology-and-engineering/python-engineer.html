About Internship with TORCHE

Internship with TORCHE is a 4-months remote internship program for students and fresh graduates to kickstart their career in education technology company. Please note that this is an unpaid internship with benefits and incentives to support the internship programs. The expected work hour for the internship program is 14 hours/week or 2 hours/day (including weekends), and can be freely arranged by each interns.

Torche
